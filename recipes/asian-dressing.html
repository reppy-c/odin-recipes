Asian Sesame Salad Dressing

Description

Use this Asian dressing for leafy green salads, Asian slaws, noodle salads and chopped salads. Itâ€™s also a terrific dressing to make steamed vegetables so much tastier. Finish with a sprinkle of fresh sesame seeds and sliced greens onions!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 2-3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons brown or granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Chill.
  3. Serve.
